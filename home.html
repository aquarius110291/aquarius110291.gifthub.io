INTRODUCTION

A profoundly Roman fragrance made of precisely chosen ingredients of the highest quality. A soft, smoky blend comes from the woody elegance of cedar and vetiver essences.
This blend is shaken by the oriental sensuality of vanilla accord and iris to give an unexpected strength of character.
A self-celebration of masculine elegance from Roma.

DESIGN

The bottle is designed with the iconic Valentino stud, inspired by Roman architecture, which is the signature of Valentino Couture.
